Viva Questions and Answers

Q1. What is the title of your project?

Answer:
Dark Pattern Impact Analytics

--------------------------------------------------

Q2. What is the objective of this project?

Answer:
To analyze application trustworthiness and identify potential dark pattern impacts using trust scores, risk analysis and business intelligence techniques.

--------------------------------------------------

Q3. Why did you choose this project?

Answer:
Dark patterns affect user trust and decision-making. This project helps evaluate application reliability and transparency.

--------------------------------------------------

Q4. Which technologies were used?

Answer:
- PostgreSQL
- SQL
- Power BI
- Microsoft Word
- GitHub

--------------------------------------------------

Q5. What datasets were used?

Answer:
- Applications Dataset
- Reviews Dataset
- Dark Patterns Dataset
- Trust Scores Dataset

--------------------------------------------------

Q6. What is a Dark Pattern?

Answer:
A Dark Pattern is a deceptive UI/UX design practice that manipulates users into making unintended decisions.

--------------------------------------------------

Q7. What are examples of Dark Patterns?

Answer:
- Hidden Costs
- Forced Continuity
- Confirm Shaming
- Privacy Zuckering
- Roach Motel
- Bait and Switch

--------------------------------------------------

Q8. How many applications were analyzed?

Answer:
6 Applications

--------------------------------------------------

Q9. How many reviews were analyzed?

Answer:
600 Reviews

Q12. What is Risk Classification?

Answer:
A method used to categorize applications into Low Risk, Medium Risk and High Risk groups based on trust scores.

Q13. Why was Power BI used?

Answer:
To create dashboards and visualize analytical insights interactively.

--------------------------------------------------

Q14. What are the benefits of this project?

Answer:

- Identifies trust issues
- Provides business recommendations

--------------------------------------------------

Answer:
Database design, SQL analytics, trust score analysis, dashboard development, reporting and documentation.

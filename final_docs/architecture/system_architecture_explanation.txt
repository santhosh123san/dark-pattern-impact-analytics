System Architecture Explanation

Project:
Dark Pattern Impact Analytics

System Components

1. Applications Layer

Purpose:
Stores application information.

Table:
apps

Fields:
- app_id
- app_name
- category
- platform
- analysis_date

--------------------------------------------------

2. Reviews Layer

Purpose:
Stores application reviews.

Table:
reviews

Fields:
- review_id
- app_id
- review_text
- sentiment

--------------------------------------------------

3. Dark Patterns Layer

Purpose:
Stores dark pattern categories.

Table:
darkpatterns

Examples:
- Hidden Costs
- Forced Continuity
- Confirm Shaming
- Privacy Zuckering
- Roach Motel
- Bait and Switch

--------------------------------------------------

4. Trust Analytics Layer

Purpose:
Calculates trust scores.

Table:
trustscores

Outputs:
- Trust Score
- Risk Level

--------------------------------------------------

5. Analytics Layer

Purpose:
Generate business insights.

Outputs:
- Risk Analysis
- Reliability Analysis
- Strategic Insights
- Recommendations

--------------------------------------------------

6. Visualization Layer

Tool:
Power BI

Outputs:
- KPI Cards
- Charts
- Risk Visualizations
- Trust Score Dashboards

--------------------------------------------------

Architecture Flow

Apps
  |
  v
Reviews
  |
  v
Dark Patterns
  |
  v
Trust Score Calculation
  |
  v
Risk Classification
  |
  v
Business Analytics
  |
  v
Power BI Dashboard

Conclusion

The architecture converts raw application data into actionable business intelligence and trust analytics.
